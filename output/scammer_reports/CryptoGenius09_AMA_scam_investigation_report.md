## 🔐 Prevention for Future

### Lessons Learned:

**1. Verified Accounts Can Scam**
- Verification badge ≠ legitimacy
- Scammers can build long-term legitimate-looking presence
- Always verify services before paying

**2. Service-Based Scams**
- Common pattern: Pay for AMA/giveaway → no delivery
- Always get refund policy in writing
- Use escrow for larger amounts
- Get references from other projects

**3. Due Diligence Checklist**
Before paying for AMA/giveaway:
- [ ] Get refund policy in writing
- [ ] Check for previous scam reports
- [ ] Ask for references (other AMAs hosted)
- [ ] Use escrow for larger amounts ($100+)
- [ ] Ask for payment confirmation screenshots
- [ ] Get date/time commitment in writing
- [ ] Verify account history
- [ ] Check for other victim reports
- [ ] Start with small test payments ($10-20)

**4. Agentic Bro Improvement:**
- Add AMA/giveaway scam detection to red flags
- Update risk scoring to account for service fraud
- Track verified service scammers separately
- Provide service verification service
- Add "Service Provider Scam" category to database

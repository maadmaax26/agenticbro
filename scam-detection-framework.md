
## 🤖 Telegram Bot Scam Indicators (New Enhancement)

Bot scams often utilize 'growth tool' or 'bump bot' branding. Key red flags:
1. **Unsolicited DM/Promotion:** Bots that automatically join groups or send DMs.
2. **Artificial Engagement:** Promising 'growth' or 'boosts' that require connecting a wallet.
3. **Hidden Fees/Draining:** Bot commands requiring high 'gas' or fees for basic services.
4. **No Transparent Code:** Proprietary, closed-source bot logic.
5. **Community Sentiment:** Search for recent Reddit/X complaints about the specific bot name.
6. **Fake 'Verified' Status:** Claims of being 'official' for major platforms (like Pump.fun or DexScreener).

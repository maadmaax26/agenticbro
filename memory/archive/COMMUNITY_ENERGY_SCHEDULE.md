# AgenticBro Community Energy Schedule

**Purpose:** Create positive, upward growth vibe with morning energy and active engagement periods

---

## Posting Schedule

### Morning Energy (7:00 AM - 9:00 AM EST)
**Goal:** Wake up the community with energy, motivation, and opportunity

| Time | Post Type | Content |
|------|------------|---------|
| 7:00 AM | Morning Energy | Motivation + market outlook + daily opportunity |
| 7:30 AM | Educational | Scam tip of the day (short, actionable) |
| 8:00 AM | Community | Engagement question or poll |
| 8:30 AM | Value Add | Quick tip or insight (scan tip, security tip) |

### Midday Momentum (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM EST)
**Goal:** Keep energy high during lunch break, drive engagement

| Time | Post Type | Content |
|------|------------|---------|
| 12:00 PM | Midday Check-in | Market update + community question |
| 12:30 PM | Educational | Deeper educational content |
| 1:00 PM | Community | Member spotlight or success story |
| 1:30 PM | Action | Call to action (scan request, share experience) |

## Engagement Tactics

### Questions That Drive Comments
- "What's your biggest crypto security concern right now?"
- "Have you ever almost fell for a scam? What stopped you?"
- "What feature would make AgenticBro more useful for you?"
- "What's the most common scam you see in your DMs?"
- "If you could scan anything for scams, what would it be?"

### Calls to Action
- "Drop a 🦞 if you're online right now!"
- "Tag someone who needs to see this"
- "Reply with your scan count this week"
- "Share this with someone who's been scammed"

### Contests/Games
- "First 3 to comment get a free priority scan"
- "Spot the scam: Which of these is fake?"
- "Guess the risk score: I'll reveal in 1 hour"

## Response Guidelines

**When community members comment:**
- Like and reply within 30 minutes during active hours
- Tag the member in reply
- Ask follow-up questions
- Provide value in response

**Example responses:**
- "Great question @[member]! Here's what I found..."
- "Thanks for sharing @[member]! That's exactly why we..."
- "Solid point @[member]! What do you think about..."

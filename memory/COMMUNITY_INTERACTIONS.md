## Security Reminders

### For Community Members
1. Never share private keys or seed phrases
2. Always verify contract addresses
3. Scan profiles before trusting
4. Report suspicious activity
5. Join official channels only

### Red Flags to Warn About
- "Guaranteed returns" - Always a scam
- "Private alpha" - High risk
- "Act now, limited time" - Pressure tactics
- "Send crypto to receive more" - Classic scam
- Unverified contracts - Always verify

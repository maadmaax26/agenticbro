## Brand Guard Overview

Brand Guard is AgenticBro's B2B subscription service that **protects businesses and companies** from brand impersonation attacks:
- Email spoof detection (SPF/DKIM/DMARC) — prevent phishing from fake sender addresses
- Lookalike domain monitoring (Certificate Transparency) — catch typosquatting and impersonator domains
- Impersonator detection (social media fake accounts) — find fake profiles scamming your customers
- Real-time alerts via email + in-app — know immediately when threats appear

**Who it protects:**
- **Any business with an online presence** — not just crypto
- Crypto projects & token teams
- DeFi protocols & DAOs
- NFT collections & creators
- E-commerce brands
- SaaS companies
- Financial services & fintech
- Gaming studios
- Influencers & public figures
- Law firms, agencies, consultancies
- Any company that sends email to customers

**Why businesses need it:**
- Scammers impersonate brands to steal from customers
- Email spoofing damages sender reputation
- Lookalike domains drain user funds
- Social media fakes spread misinformation
- Brand reputation takes years to build, minutes to destroy

## $AGNTCBRO Buyback Program

**Purpose:** Brand Guard subscription revenue supports the $AGNTCBRO ecosystem through periodic buybacks.

### How It Works

1. **Revenue Collection:** Monthly Brand Guard subscription payments (net of operating costs)
2. **Buyback Allocation:** 30-50% of net revenue allocated for $AGNTCBRO purchases
3. **Execution:** Buybacks executed on-chain via Jupiter aggregator
4. **Transparency:** On-chain transactions published to community

### Buyback Schedule

| Frequency | Minimum Threshold | Announcement |
|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| Monthly | $500 accumulated | Pre-announcement 24h before |
| Quarterly | Any amount | Published in quarterly report |

### Allocation of Purchased Tokens

| Use | Percentage |
|-----|------------|
| **Burn** | 50% — permanently removed from supply |
| **Treasury** | 30% — held for future development |
| **Community Rewards** | 20% — contests, airdrops, incentives |

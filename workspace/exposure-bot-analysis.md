## How Exposure Bots Work

### Mechanism
1. **Trending Detection:** Bot monitors trending tokens/projects
2. **Channel Promotion:** Pushes token information to all group members
3. **Affiliate Tracking:** Uses referral links to track conversions/commissions
4. **Exposure Duration:** Typically 24-48 hours of visibility

### Typical Workflow
```
Trending Token → Bot Detects → Channel Announcement → Member Views → (Optional) Member Trades → Affiliate Commission
```

## Scam Detection for @majorboostbot

### High Risk Indicators
- ❌ No transparent website
- ❌ Large developer wallet holding 50%+ token supply
- ❌ High buy/sell tax (10%+)
- ❌ Liquidity unlocked or not locked
- ❌ No clear team identity
- ❌ "Guaranteed results" or "100% ROI" promises
- ❌ No track record of successful promotions
- ❌ Urgency tactics ("act now, only 24h!")

### Low Risk Indicators
- ✅ Transparent website and team
- ✅ Liquidity locked (CertiK, PinkSale, etc.)
- ✅ Tokenomics: small dev wallet, fair distribution
- ✅ Moderate taxes (0-5%)
- ✅ Proven track record of successful promotions
- ✅ Clear communication and responsiveness
